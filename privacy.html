Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to CoTerm ("we" or "the App"). We understand the importance of personal information to you and will do our best to protect your personal information securely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide our services:

  • Device Identifier - Used to identify your device and sync subscription status
  • Usage Data - Anonymous statistics to help us improve product experience
  • Crash Reports - Used to identify and fix technical issues
  • Subscription Information - Purchase records handled through Apple App Store

Terminal Content Privacy

Your terminal session content (including commands, output, file content, etc.) is only used to interact with AI models to complete tasks. We commit:

  • Will not store your terminal content on our servers
  • Will not use your code or data to train AI models
  • Will not sell or share your terminal content with third parties

Third-Party Services

To enable core features, we use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic API - Provides Claude AI model services, subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI API - Optional AI model service, subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store - Handles in-app purchases and subscriptions, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Transport layer encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted local data storage
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection regulations, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to withdraw consent

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted in the App, and we will notify you of material changes through the App.
